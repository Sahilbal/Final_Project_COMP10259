World Count

Sea turtle populations differ between species.Our oceans are home to seven different species of sea turtles. While some sea turtle populations are incredibly prosperous, others have difficulty engaging in critical thought. It is difficult to estimate the calculation precisely. Since male sea turtles never leave the water during the hatching season, it is impossible to determine the real population size of these animals. Nonetheless, by keeping an eye on female sea turtles as they nest, we can gain a decent understanding of the population, as 90% of sea turtles are actually female. In light of that, the nesting population can be calculated as follows:
